autumnary Privacy Policy

Last Revision: January 3, 2014

This Privacy Policy is intended to explain how personal information is used in relation to autumnary the mobile and tablet application (“App”) and the website (“Site”).

As the product grows in features and usefulness, our Privacy Policy will be updated along with the date of the latest update to the document. If you are not comfortable with our policies, we suggest not using autumnary. Perhaps consider a relaxing stroll through your nearest park instead.

We are Objectify LLC, a company that strives to turn raw concepts into tangible products and makers of autumnary, a service that creates engaging audio experiences in mobile applications.

WHAT WE COLLECT

We collect the sounds you interact with through the App (“Sound(s)”). A Sound is a collection of information that helps us recreate the circumstances at that particular time you used the App.

Weather and neighborhood information may be part of a Sound you interact with. We gather the weather and neighborhood you were in through collection of your location.

When accessing the Site, we collect your browser data, IP address, and cookies. Login information used in conjunction with the App is also collected.

We may also collect aggregate user data to refine and better understand overall usage of the App. We do not share this information with a third party unless legally obligated to do so.

WHAT YOU AND OTHERS SHARE

The App allows you to share your name and favorite sounds to social media sites. Actions taken involving these third party sites are voluntary and outside of our control. As such, please use best judgment when sharing.
